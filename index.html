King Amanollah Khan

King Amanollah Khan, 1927

King Amanollah Khan was the ruler of Afghanistan from 1919 to 1929. He is known for his efforts in modernizing Afghanistan, his diplomatic initiatives, and his attempts to build a more progressive society. He declared Afghanistan's independence from British influence in 1919 and introduced numerous social, cultural, and economic reforms.

During his reign, he worked towards reducing the influence of religious leaders and implementing western-inspired policies. Despite facing significant resistance and eventually being overthrown, his legacy remains as a progressive and visionary leader who sought to bring Afghanistan into the modern era.
